Resources for teaching in your local community

Dear teachers,

We are delighted to welcome you to Soma Nyumbani program. In this challenging time, we recognise more than ever the need to provide high quality support to teachers implement community-based learning. We aim to provide free and open education activities for secondary school teachers and students inline with the government guidelines.

As such please find below 4 suggested activities:

  • Session 1- Counting squares activity
  • Session 2- Mirroring moves
  • Session 3- Understanding COVID-19 Prevention
  • Session 4- Creative Writing
