Resources for teaching mathematics in your local community

Following the directive from the Teacher Service Commision, INNODEMS plans to offer teachers across Kenya free and open Mathematics Education resources to use for Lower Primary, Upper Primary and Secondary students. Our hope is that these resources will provide teachers with engaging activities suitable for students of all ages within each group.

Materials for each category will be added daily with details on how these can be used. These will include:

  • online questions with instantaneous feedback
  • an automated chatbot to use with Telegram,
  • pdfs with instructions,
  • videos, and
  • podcasts.

If you would like additional information or would like regular updates please fill in this form to let us know.

If you are interested in contributing to the effort of creating materials please fill in this form to let us know.

If you have any feedback about any of our activities please contact us. Your opinion is crucial for the successful design of further activities.
